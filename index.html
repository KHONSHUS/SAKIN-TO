My Life's Journey

Film day

Shooting a project, March 22, 2025

We filmed our project to show the effects of the continuous problems happening at school — especially the issue of unfair grading. During filming, we shared real situations and personal experiences that many students face but are often ignored. We worked together to act out scenes that show how unfair grading can affect a student's confidence and motivation. It was challenging but meaningful — we had to plan every scene carefully to make sure it reflected the reality in school. Filming helped me realize that this issue is not just about grades, but also about how students are treated. Through this project, we were able to express our feelings and speak up about something that really matters to us.

Developing system

Implementing library management software, October 12, 2024

We worked together to build a system that would not only meet the requirements, but also stand out. Throughout the process, we helped one another — reviewing codes, fixing errors, and constantly improving our project. One of the most important parts of our journey was brainstorming. We shared ideas, discussed problems, and explored possible solutions together as a team. It wasn’t always easy, but the collaboration made the process smoother and more meaningful. As time went on, we saw how our efforts started to pay off. We were proud to be selected as part of the CITE Expo, and even more proud when we were chosen as one of the Top 3 teams. This experience taught us the value of teamwork, persistence, and believing in what we can achieve together.
